Review iPhone 13 Pro Max – Unparalleled performance, extreme camera

iPhone 12 launched not long ago, the latest rumors about iPhone 13 Pro Max have made many technology followers wait. The upgraded camera cluster, new colors, especially the 120Hz screen with a more compact notch are the points that make it all the more attractive this year.

Elegant flat edge design, colorful highlights

The iPhone 12 series is applied by Apple with the same design language as last year's iPhone 12 with luxurious flat edges and four slightly curved corners, this can be considered a nostalgic design from the iPhone line. 5 earlier. Therefore, the iPhone 13 Pro Max in particular, as well as the iPhone 13 series in general, will also follow this design language.

The big change on the 13 Pro Max may be the protrusion of the camera cluster. Meanwhile, the front of the device is covered with a layer of Ceramic Shield, a material that is harder than any smartphone glass currently on the market, which effectively protects the iPhone screen.

Screen with smaller ears, refresh rate 120 Hz

One of the factors that makes the iPhone 13 Pro Max desirable is the reduced "rabbit ears" notch design. In addition to the 6.7-inch screen size with Super Retina XDR OLED panel, the device will have a narrowed notch design, helping to increase the display ratio on the phone screen. Of course, important sensors like TrueDepth, Face ID or selfie camera will all stay in place.
